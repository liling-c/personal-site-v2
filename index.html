About Me.

Welcome to my website. I'm a recent graduate from Boston University who somehow stumbled her way into becoming a programmer. As a kid, I spent my time drawing and folding origami. I told my parents I wanted to be an artist when I grew up; my interest in programming began with a happy accident. In 10th grade, I was placed into a programming class instead of the drawing elective I requested. I wondered, "What is programming?" I attended it and haven't looked back.

I look forward to what new things I'll gain each day and hope to acquire the necessary skills to combine all my interests into one project. In my spare time, when I'm not staring at a code editor, I enjoy playing Genshin Impact, drawing (read: staring at a blank piece of paper) and hoarding stationery.
